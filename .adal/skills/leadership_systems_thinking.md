# Leadership & Systems Thinking Skills

## Core Technologies & Competencies
- **Systems Thinking**: Viewing software not as isolated applications, but as interconnected ecosystems; optimizing for the whole lifecycle rather than local silos.
- **Servant Leadership**: Leading without formal authority, mentoring peers, and pulling the organization forward by sharing purpose and vision.
- **Conflict Resolution & Alignment**: Navigating healthy conflict constructively, challenging existing governance models, and aligning diverse stakeholders around pragmatic yet ambitious outcomes.
- **Business Awareness**: Connecting technical initiatives directly to business value, measuring success through cost avoidance, risk reduction, and improved delivery outcomes.
- **Constructive Disruption**: Deliberately positioning the ecosystem for a future where engineers operate as architects of automation rather than manual implementers.
